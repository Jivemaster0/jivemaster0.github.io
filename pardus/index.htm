Pardus

Elite was the first ever game two friends and I purchased the day it was released for our Acorn BBC Model B computers. We had a race to see who would be "Elite" first. I did not win because I was not allowed to keep my computer on for more than 24 hours a day.

One copy between the three of us you may ask, :), that is another story. Those were the days of real computing, 6502 machine code, 6522 VIAs, peeks and pokes, ...

I now enjoy Pardus, so I thought I might as well help others out with information that I found useful.

Infocenter - Combat logger

Many thanks to Pio for releasing this tool. It has enabled people to share their experience to a wider audience. My implementation is called the Union Infocenter (Jivemaster) and it is available at http://www.jivemaster.byethost4.com/pardus/infocenter/index.php to Artemis-Union players. Give me a shout ingame if you need access or encounter any problems.

Get the latest upload script from http://www.jivemaster.byethost4.com/pardus/gmscripts. The script you want is pardus_infocenter_share.user.js. If you already have the script, you can add my database to the list of servers at the head of the script.
